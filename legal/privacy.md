# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 9, 2026

## What We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Account details (name, email, business name)
- Chatbot content you upload
- Knowledge base documents
- Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

### Automatically Collected
- Usage data (conversations, features used)
- Device and browser information
- Cookies for session management

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Process payments
- Communicate with you
- Analyze usage to improve our product
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Protection

- All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- We follow industry security standards
- Regular security audits

## Third-Party Services

We use trusted third parties:
- **Stripe** - Payment processing
- **OpenAI/Anthropic** - AI services (your conversations)
- **SendClaw** - Email delivery

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request data deletion
- Export your data
- Opt-out of marketing emails

## Data Retention

- Account data: As long as account is active
- Conversation logs: 12 months
- Payment records: 7 years (legal requirement)

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not for children under 13.
