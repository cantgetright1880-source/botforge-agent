# BotForge - Free Business Tools

## 📧 Free Business Email

### Option 1: Zoho Mail (RECOMMENDED)
- **Free:** Up to 5 mailboxes
- **Custom domain:** yes
- **Storage:** 5GB per user
- **Setup:** zoho.com/mail
- **Pros:** Professional, secure, no ads

### Option 2: Cloudflare Email Routing
- **Free:** Unlimited aliases
- **Forward only:** Yes (no sending)
- **Cost:** Free
- **Setup:** cloudflare.com → Email
- **Pros:** Totally free, unlimited

### Option 3: Forward Email
- **Free:** Unlimited forwarding
- **Custom domain:** Yes
- **Cost:** Free (donations accepted)
- **Setup:** forwardemail.net
